With its on-trend satchel silhouette, the Pochette Métis bag has become an object of fashion desire. This compact, go-everywhere model in the House’s Monogram canvas features a distinctive S-lock closure with a polished gold-tone finish. Versatile and unexpectedly roomy, it is fitted with a chic top handle and a long detachable strap for shoulder or cross-body wear.

Detailed features

  • Textile lining
  • Gold-color hardware
  • Short top handle for hand carry
  • Removable, adjustable strap (drop: min. 48 cm/ 18.9 inches, max. 55 cm/21.7 inches) for shoulder or cross-body carry
  • S-lock closure

  • To keep your Monogram canvas product beautiful as the years pass, we recommend following these guidelines for its care :

    - Beware not to scratch or rub your product against abrasive surfaces, especially the leather trim.
    - Keep your product away from damp or humid environments and avoid direct exposure to sunlight, keep your product away from any direct source of heat (radiators, car interiors overheated by the sun, etc).
    - Avoid contact with greasy substances, cosmetics, perfume, and hydroalcoholic solutions, as well as any material (magazines, other leathers, etc.) that may transfer their coloured pigments onto the product.
    - Keep your product away from water. Should it get wet or dirty on the surface, dry with a lint free, light-coloured, absorbent cloth. Never use soap or solvent.
    - If your lining gets dirty or in case of superficial stains, we recommend that you wipe it with a soft and light-coloured cloth.
    - In order to protect your product when you are not using it, store it in the felt protective pouch provided.

    Natural cowhide leather:

    The trimmings are in natural cowhide leather, the skin is finished through a vegetal tanning process. Some natural marks or genuine irregularities in the leather may show through. Over time, this delicate leather, which is sensitive to scratches, will acquire a beautiful patina.
    Taking proper care of your Louis Vuitton product will allow you to fully appreciate its beauty for many years.

    For any enquiries about your product, please do not hesitate to contact any Louis Vuitton store.

  • Delivery details

    Louis Vuitton only offers delivery in the country where the order has been placed online or by phone.

    Standard Delivery:
    The expected time for standard delivery is 3 to 5 business days following order placement, except for "Mon Monogram" products, which we aim to deliver within 8 weeks of the order being placed. Shipping charges for standard delivery orders are complimentary.
    *For shipments to remote and rural areas including, but not limited to regional Northern Territory, Queensland and Western Australia addresses please allow up to 7 business days for delivery.

    Next Day Delivery:
    If overnight delivery is requested, your order will be prioritised and will arrive the next business day (Mon-Fri excluding public holidays) from the time you receive email shipping confirmation. Please note overnight orders placed after 2:00 PM AEST, or anytime Saturday or Sunday may not be processed until the following business day. This service is only available to selected postcodes and is not available for Mon Monogram orders.

    Same Business Day Delivery:
    If same business day delivery is requested, your order will be prioritised with expected arrival before 6pm on the same business day (Mon-Fri excluding public holidays) you receive an email shipping confirmation. Please note same business day orders placed after 12:00 PM AEST, or anytime Saturday or Sunday may not be processed until the following business day. This service is presently only available to selected Sydney Metropolitan post codes and is not available for Mon Monogram orders.
    *A special late delivery between 6pm and 8pm is available within selected Sydney metropolitan postcodes by contacting Client Services on 1300 582 827.

    Please note that Overnight and Same Business Day Delivery is not available for Watches and Jewellery products.

    Town name should be entered in the "City" field and match exactly with the postcode.
